2. Scope of this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the CommunityHelp Android application and the related backend services used to operate the application.

3. Personal data processed

Depending on how CommunityHelp is used, the following categories of personal data may be processed:

  • Account information, including name, email address, authentication details and user ID
  • Profile information, including introduction, availability, selected help categories and ratings
  • Contact information, including telephone number
  • Location information, including postcode, town, approximate coordinates and, where required for an accepted request, an exact address
  • Help-request information, including task description, requested date, arrival window and estimated duration
  • Offers to help, including proposed arrival time and optional messages
  • Private chat messages exchanged between participants in an accepted request
  • Ratings, reports, safety information and blocking information
  • Technical information, including device identifiers, push-notification tokens, timestamps and diagnostic information

4. Purposes of processing

Personal data is processed for the following purposes:

  • Creating and managing user accounts
  • Publishing and displaying local help requests
  • Matching people requesting help with suitable helpers
  • Allowing users to offer, accept and manage help
  • Enabling private communication between participants
  • Sharing necessary contact and address information after a helper has been selected
  • Sending push notifications about messages, offers, request updates and other relevant activity
  • Supporting ratings, reports, blocking and community safety
  • Preventing misuse and maintaining the security and reliability of the service

5. Legal bases

Where the General Data Protection Regulation applies, processing may be based on one or more of the following legal bases:

  • Article 6(1)(b) GDPR, where processing is necessary to provide the requested app functions
  • Article 6(1)(f) GDPR, where processing is necessary for legitimate interests such as service security, misuse prevention and reliable operation
  • Article 6(1)(a) GDPR, where consent is requested for a specific processing activity
  • Article 6(1)(c) GDPR, where processing is necessary to comply with a legal obligation

6. Public and private information

Public request listings are intended to show only the information necessary for other community members to understand a request. Exact addresses, private telephone numbers and private email addresses are not intended to be displayed publicly.

Exact address and contact information may be made available to the selected helper after an offer has been accepted, where this is necessary to carry out the requested help.

7. Firebase and Google services

CommunityHelp uses services provided by Google Firebase, including:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Cloud Firestore
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging
  • Google Cloud Functions

These services are used for account authentication, database storage, backend processing and push notifications.

Google may process technical and service-related data in accordance with its applicable terms and privacy documentation.

8. Maps and location services

CommunityHelp may use Google Maps Platform and Google Places services to search for, select and display locations.

Information entered into address-search or map functions may be transmitted to Google for the purpose of providing those services.

9. Push notifications

CommunityHelp uses Firebase Cloud Messaging to send notifications about relevant app activity.

A device token is stored for this purpose. Users may disable notification permissions through the Android system settings. Disabling notifications does not delete the user account or other stored app data.

10. Data sharing

Personal data is not sold.

Data may be disclosed only where necessary:

  • To other users as part of the intended CommunityHelp functionality
  • To service providers that process data for the operation of the app
  • To authorities or other recipients where disclosure is legally required
  • To protect users, rights, security or the integrity of the service

11. International data transfers

Some service providers may process data outside Germany or outside the European Economic Area.

Where required, appropriate safeguards are used in accordance with applicable data-protection law.

12. Data retention

Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

The exact retention period may depend on the type of data, the status of a help request, account activity, safety requirements and legal obligations.

  • Request access to their personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of personal data
  • Request restriction of processing
  • Object to certain processing
  • Request data portability
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent
  • Lodge a complaint with a competent data-protection authority

Requests may be sent to: rory.pittman@outlook.de

CommunityHelp does not knowingly intend to collect personal data from children without an appropriate legal basis or, where required, valid parental consent.
